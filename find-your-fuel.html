Espresso

Tea

Matcha

The Breakdown

Coffee is great for busy professionals, early risers, or anyone needing a quick energy boost. With around 95 mg of caffeine per 8 oz, it delivers fast, steady energy but can cause jitters or crashes if over-consumed. Its bold taste may not be for everyone.

Tea provides a slower, steadier lift with 30-70 mg of caffeine per 8 oz. It’s perfect for those who enjoy a calming ritual and prefer consistent energy throughout the day. Its earthy flavors can be an acquired taste.

Energy Drinks drinks are designed for athletes, night owls, or anyone needing an intense, rapid boost. With 80-300 mg of caffeine per can, they provide instant energy but may lead to crashes and often contain sugar or artificial ingredients. They’re best for those who need quick, powerful fuel.
